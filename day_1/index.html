Lemon Pie

Description

A lemon pie, also known as lemon meringue pie, is a classic dessert renowned for its tangy yet sweet flavor profile and delightful texture

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pie crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 4 large egg yolks, lightly beaten
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Roll out the pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie dish. Crimp the edges of the crust as desired, and then prick the bottom and sides with a fork to prevent air bubbles from forming during baking. Bake the crust according to package instructions or until golden brown. Allow it to cool completely before adding the filling.
  3. In a medium saucepan, whisk together 1 1/4 cups sugar, cornstarch, and salt.
  4. Gradually whisk in the water until smooth, then add the egg yolks, lemon zest, and lemon juice.
  5. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. This usually takes about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the butter until melted and well combined.
  7. Pour the lemon filling into the cooled pie crust.
  8. In a clean, dry mixing bowl, beat the egg whites and cream of tartar on high speed until soft peaks form.
  9. Gradually add 1/2 cup of sugar, a tablespoon at a time, while continuing to beat until stiff peaks form and the sugar is dissolved.
  10. Spread the meringue over the hot lemon filling, making sure to spread it all the way to the edges of the crust to seal in the filling.
  11. Use the back of a spoon to create decorative peaks in the meringue.
  12. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the meringue is lightly golden brown.
  13. Allow the pie to cool completely at room temperature for at least 2 hours before slicing and serving.
  14. Once cooled, slice the pie into wedges and serve. Enjoy!
